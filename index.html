How to play

Place one crown in every row, column, and colour region.

  • Exactly one ♛ per row, per column, and per colour region.
  • Crowns may never touch — not even diagonally.
  • Tap a cell once to mark it ✕ (can't be a crown), tap again to clear.
  • Double-tap a cell to place or remove a crown.

Every level has one solution you can reach with logic alone — no guessing.
